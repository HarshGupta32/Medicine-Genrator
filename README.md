In order to make prescription pricing retrieval easier, this study presents a solution that combines OCR technology with a welcoming Chabot interface. Prescriptions are photographed by users, text is extracted via OCR, and real-time pricing recommendations are obtained by the Chabot from a drug database. Experiments validate its efficiency in streamlining cost access. Future improvements might focus on improving healthcare affordability and accessibility by offering customised services and bilingual assistance. With this integration, users wil lbe able to make better medical decisions and perhaps enhance their quality of life and healthcare outcomes. This is a big leap in healthcare technology
